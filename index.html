Dashboard Overview

Use the interactive graphics on this page to explore the belly button dataset


Pick a "Test Subject ID No." from the drop down menu to see their test results. The demographic info panel and visuals will populate to reflect the information associated with the new ID.

There are three different visuals displayed on the dashboard:

  1. Bar Chart: Top 10 bacteria species for the selected subject. Samples are sorted in descending order based on sample value
  2. Bubble Chart: All bacteria samples for the selected subject. The bubble size reflects the sample value. The bubble placement on the x-axis and color are based on the taxonomic unit.
  3. Gauge Dial: Represents the number of times a week the selected subject reported washing their belly button. Some subjects reported a score of 0, in which case the gauge indicator will not be visible.
